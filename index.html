SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISE BUSINESS (SMEB) PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Supply Chain Financing
Brief:
  1. This product is backed by Anchors.
  2. The Anchor typically provides comfort in below forms
    1. First Loss Default Guarantee (FLDG in combination of Inter-corporate desposits, Bank Guarantee and Corporate Guarantee)
    2. Corporate Guarantee
    3. Stop Supply / Co-lending
  3. Tenor varies from upto 120 days revolving depending on actual credit period extended.
  4. Mechanism â€“ Purchase and Sales Financing is explained in next slides.
Mechanism - Purchase Finance:
  1. Vendor supplies goods and provides invoice to anchor.
  2. Vendor applies to lender for finance.
  3. Anchor provides acceptance with respect to invoice to lender
  4. Lender disburses the amount to vendor after deducting upfront interest.
  5. On the due date (depending on the credit period), anchor makes payment to lender.
Mechanism - Sales Financing:
  1. Anchor supplies goods / service to Customer.
  2. Anchor provides accepted invoice to Lender .
  3. Lender funds Anchor post receiving accepted invoice after deducting upfront interest.
  4. Lender disburses the amount to vendor after deducting upfront interest.
  5. Customer pays Lender at the end of credit period.
Hospitality Financing
Brief:
  1. This product focuses on financing to Mid Market/Budget Hotels driven by renowned brands.
  2. Ticket Size Varies from Rs. 5 lakhs to Rs. 500 lakhs.
  3. End use of these loans are working capital finance and upgradation/expansion of existing hotels.
  4. Tenor for term loans is upto 5 years and working capital is upto 3 yrs.
  5. Exclusive program is set up for anchor backed unsecured loans for faster approvals and loan processing.
Educational Institutions Financing
Brief:
  1. This product focuses on financing to recognized K-12 schools.
  2. Ticket Size Varies from Rs. 25 lakhs to Rs. 300 lakhs.
  3. Finance is offered towards creation, upgrade or expansion of educational infrastructure or facilities or towards funding working capital gap.
  4. Term Loans upto 7 years in an EMI strcuture with property or liquid collateral.
  5. Exclusive program for faster approvals and loan processing.
